Ordering Process

How to Order

  1. Browse products and add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout when ready
  3. Enter shipping and payment information
  4. Review and confirm your order
  5. Receive order confirmation via email

Pricing & Payments

All prices are in Indian Rupees (₹) and inclusive of all applicable taxes. We accept:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • UPI Payments
  • Net Banking
  • Wallet Payments (PayTM, PhonePe, etc.)

Your account will be charged when your order is confirmed.

Order Changes & Cancellations

Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email if you need to change or cancel your order. Changes may be possible if your order hasn't shipped yet.

Return Policy

Our Policy

We accept returns within 3 days of delivery. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original condition with tags attached
  • In original packaging

How to Return

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select "Return" for the item
  4. We'll arrange free pickup (for prepaid orders)
  5. For COD returns, please ship via India Post

Refund Process

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect your return.

For COD returns, refunds will be processed via bank transfer (Please provide IFSC code and account details).

Exchanges

For size exchanges, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired size.

Defective Products

For defective items, please contact us within 48 hours of receipt at returns@samlara.com with photos/video of the issue.
