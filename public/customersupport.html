Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

We process orders within 5-7 day (including weekends and holidays). Shipping times vary:

  • Tricity: 1-2 days
  • Out of tricity: 5-7 days
  • Rest of locations: 7-10 days

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide via DHL or USPS International Priority. Please note:

  • Customs fees may apply
  • Shipping times vary by destination
  • Prices are in USD and exclude import duties

Can I track my order?

Yes! Tracking information is emailed to you immediately after shipping. You can also:

  1. Log into your SamLara account
  2. Click the tracking link in your confirmation email
  3. Visit the carrier's website with your tracking number

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 3 days after delivery:

  • Items must be unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Underwear and swimwear are final sale

To initiate a return, visit the Returns Center in your account.

How do exchanges work?

Our exchange process is simple:

  1. Return your original item following our standard return process
  2. Place a new order for the correct size/color
  3. We'll process your refund once we receive the return

Exchanges are subject to inventory availability.

Product & Sizing

How do I choose the right size?

We recommend checking our detailed size charts on each product page:

  • Measure your chest, waist, and hips
  • Compare to our size chart measurements
  • Consider your preferred fit (slim/regular/loose)

Contact us for personalized recommendations.

What materials are your t-shirts made from?

We use premium materials:

  • 100% organic ring-spun cotton
  • Tri-blend (polyester/cotton/rayon) for softness
  • Moisture-wicking performance fabrics

All materials are Oeko-Tex certified for safety.
